Welcome to The Nest

We are a group of friends who decided we needed to dive deep into the Word of God and create community. In a church with thousands of people, we try to make it feel small through fellowship and genuine connection.

Our Heart

We help newcomers connect in a church with thousands by creating an intimate fellowship where everyone belongs. We don't just help you connect with people—we help you connect with different ministries inside WaterMark Church and support you with whatever needs you have.

Though most of our members are in their 20s (young adults), The Nest is designed for anyone in the community who wants to connect and create community—men and women, all ages, all backgrounds.

"And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing, but encouraging one another—and all the more as you see the Day approaching."

— Hebrews 10:24-25

Our Mission

Gather • Grow • Go

Gather

We come together every Friday evening at 7 PM to worship, fellowship, and build community. In a world that pulls us apart, we intentionally gather in Jesus' name.

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them."

— Matthew 18:20

Grow

Through teaching, small groups, and authentic conversations, we dive deep into God's Word and encourage each other to grow in faith, wisdom, and Christlike character.

"But grow in the grace and knowledge of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ."

— 2 Peter 3:18

Go

We don't just meet and grow for ourselves. We're equipped and sent out to serve others, share the Gospel, and make disciples wherever God leads us.

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations..."

— Matthew 28:19

What We Do

Every Friday night is a blend of worship, learning, and fellowship

Worship

We lift our voices together in songs of praise, creating space to encounter God's presence.

Teaching & Talks

Relevant, biblical teaching that challenges us to grow deeper in our faith and understanding.

Small Groups

Intimate discussions where we can be real, ask questions, and apply Scripture to our lives.

Activities

Fun activities, games, and fellowship events that help us connect and build lasting friendships.

Interactive Learning

Sometimes we split into groups for activities and discussions, then come back together to share our lessons learned with the larger community. This creates space for everyone's voice to be heard.

Why Friday Evenings?

As the sun sets and the week comes to a close, we gather to pause, reflect, and refocus on what matters most. Friday nights at The Nest are a time to leave the busyness behind and rest in God's presence together.

There's something special about ending the week in worship and community—it sets the tone for a weekend centered on rest, relationships, and renewed purpose.

"Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest."

— Matthew 11:28
